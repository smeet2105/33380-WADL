My Artworks

I have always been inclined towards artistic avenues. Apart from traditional sketching and portraits, I have indulged in digital art as well. Even though I'm a novice, I have tried to document my not so diverse portfolio, which comprises of posters for articles and some character designs.

This was one of my most random project. Initial layout was supposed to portray a life of gauche teenager, stuggling but trying to make everything work, but somehow I ended up making creating a monk, aware of strengths. My initial idea, and the final product are two sides of the same coin, right?

This poster was used to spread awareness on cyber-bullying, discussing how frequent it happens. More than half of the elements used in this were actually drawn.

In this poster I incorporated one of my favorite initial landscape artwork, and I'm proud of how it turned out. This was also my first poster (okay second).

Down the spine of Maharashtra was a travelogue, a 'Safarnama' documenting the serenity of my birth place. From a GTA-ish poster to diary-esque art, I saw and loved it's transformation.

I especially enojyed the opportunity to make a very technical poster. This was one of the times where I didn't run out of ideas.
